Specialties of Sri Lanka

Sri lanka is located in the Indian Ocean, south of India. It is a beautiful island nation known for its rich history, diverse culture, and stunning landscapes. The country is home to a variety of religions, including

  1. Buddhism
  2. Hinduism
  3. Islam
  4. Christianity

Sri lanka is also known for its tea plantations, ancient temples, and vibrant festivals.
